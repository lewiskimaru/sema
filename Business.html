How can I request a translation service from Sema?

To request a translation, simply visit our website and fill out the service request form with your project details. Our team will get in touch with you promptly to discuss the specifics.

What file formats do you accept for transcription services?

We accept various file formats, including MP3, MP4, WAV, and more. You can easily upload your files directly on our platform for transcription.

Can Sema's chatbots be integrated into different messaging platforms?

Yes, Sema's chatbots are designed to be seamlessly integrated into various messaging platforms, including websites, social media, and messaging apps, ensuring a smooth and consistent user experience.

How does Sema ensure cultural sensitivity in their consulting services?

Our team of experienced consultants undergoes rigorous cultural training and research to ensure a deep understanding of various cultural nuances. We prioritize cultural sensitivity in all our interactions and guidance.

Yes, we offer volume discounts for clients with recurring translation needs. Please reach out to our team for more information on our special pricing and packages for regular translation services.
